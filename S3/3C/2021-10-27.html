  • Definition of Terms

    • filtration: a method of separating an insoluble solid from a liquid or a solution
    • evaporation: a method of separarting a high boiling soluble solid from a solution
    • crystallization: a method of separating a pure soluble solid from a solution
    • saturated solution: a solution containing the maximum amount of solute at a certain temperature
